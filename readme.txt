
Livejournal Magpie
-----------------------------
Copyright (c) Angela Sabas
http://scripts.indisguise.org
=============================

Livejournal Magpie (LJ Magpie) is a tool for site owners to easily
embed a Livejournal account's most recent entries into a site.
It requires the use of Magpie RSS <http://magpierss.sourceforge.net/>.

Features:
---------
. Easily embed the latest public blog entries of a particular
  Livejournal.com account on your site
. Customize the way the entries are shown on your site


Requirements:
-------------
Magpie RSS <http://magpierss.sourceforge.net/>
PHP 4.3.0 + Magpie RSS requirements
A Livejournal.com account
